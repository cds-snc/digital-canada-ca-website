Help us fight COVID-19

We want to make government services better for everyone. But we need your help. We need to talk to real people who represent the diversity of people all across Canada.

We’re working on COVID Alert, Canada’s national exposure notification app. You can help us make it better for everyone. We want to hear from you, whether or not you’ve tried using COVID Alert.

Depending on the study, you may be eligible to receive compensation for your time.

Sign up to participate in research

If you want to participate in our research, you’ll need to answer some questions. It takes about 5 minutes. Your answers help us ensure our research represents the diversity of people across Canada. That’s why we ask for information about you, like your work, age group, ethnicity, and the technology you use.

When we need to do research, we’ll contact people who signed up to see if they can help.

Sign up now

No obligation

Signing up to participate gives us permission to contact you about research opportunities. You are under no obligation to participate. You can stop at any time.

When we start new research

If you’re chosen for research, we’ll contact you with details and answer any questions you have. If you agree to participate, the researcher will book a date and time that works for you.

If you change your mind

If you want to stop hearing about research opportunities, you can unsubscribe to the emails at any time. We’ll erase the information you gave us on the survey.

How we use and protect your information

Read the Privacy Statement for research participants.
