About us

CDS is committed to working with a range of partners to align and adapt to the evolving digital landscape and work towards a more responsive digital government as outlined in the Canada’s Digital Ambition 2024-25.

The Strategic Partnerships and Engagement Team (SPE) collaborates with a variety of external partners to help deliver CDS’ mandate. When we work with provincial, territorial and international governments we want to make sure:

  • The products and services Canada is developing can work with other jurisdictions
  • Mutually advance the development and acceptance of digital solutions
  • Learning and building from each other’s experiences
  • Building effective, efficient and trustworthy services enabled by data and technology

Along with governments, we work with academia and organizations representing traditionally underserved communities to help make it possible for CDS to chart a path forward for federal services that are innovative, equitable, and inclusive from end-to-end.
