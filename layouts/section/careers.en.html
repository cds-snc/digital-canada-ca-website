At CDS, we welcome and invite difference.

We proudly, passionately, and actively strive to make CDS more reflective of the society that we serve. We will only be able to deliver better, more accessible, and more inclusive public services if we harness diverse thoughts, experiences, and skills. We work hard to create an environment where different perspectives and experiences are valued. We are committed to helping diverse talent thrive.

CDS welcomes all applicants, including Veterans and people of all races, ethnicities, religions, sexual orientations, gender identities and expressions, national origins, disabilities, ages, body sizes and including those with diverse households and family commitments.

Our hiring process

Our goal is to find the right fit for both you and CDS. When you apply, here’s what you can generally expect:

  1. 1. Confirmation

    After you apply, you’ll get an email to let you know we’ve received your application.

  2. 2. Introductory call

    If we’d like to get to know you better, we’ll set up a brief chat to get further information.

  3. 3. Interviews

    You’ll be invited to a few video interviews:
    • Discussion with the hiring manager
    • Technical interview panel (this could include an exercise)
    • Values interview panel

  4. 4. Reference checks

    We’ll call your references to learn more about your professional experiences.

  5. 5. Job offer!

    We’ll make the formal offer and figure out when you can start.

If you don’t get offered a job, we’d be happy to discuss your application and explore future opportunities. In some cases, we’ll request to hold onto your application for future reference, as there may be a fit down the road.
